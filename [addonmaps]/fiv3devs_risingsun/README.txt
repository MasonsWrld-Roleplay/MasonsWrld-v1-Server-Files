INSTALLATION:

1) drag and drop the resource "fiv3devs_risingsun" to your resources folder.

2) start the resource   
    start fiv3devs_risingsun

3) update your server build to 5128 or higher: https://runtime.fivem.net/artifacts/fivem/

4) update your game build to 2189 or higher: "set sv_enforceGameBuild 2189" in your server.cfg

5) clear your server cache

6) DONE!

FOR MORE INFORMATION, HELP AND FREQUENT PROBLEMS:

https://fiv3devs.tebex.io/faq
